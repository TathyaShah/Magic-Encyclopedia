Hogwarts

Hogwarts is a fictional boarding school for young witches and wizards in the Scottish Highlands. It was founded in the 10th century by four powerful wizards: Godric Gryffindor, Rowena Ravenclaw, Helga Hufflepuff, and Salazar Slytherin. The school is divided into four houses: Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, and Slytherin. Students at Hogwarts study a variety of magical subjects, including Charms, Defense Against the Dark Arts, Potions, Transfiguration, and History of Magic.Hogwarts is a magical place, full of secret passages, hidden staircases, and magical creatures.
